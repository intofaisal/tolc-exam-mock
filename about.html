Our Story

How TOLC Mock Test was born

Who We Are

TOLC Mock Test was created by a group of former students who experienced firsthand the challenges of preparing for Italian university entrance exams. After struggling to find quality practice materials for the Test OnLine CISIA (TOLC) exams, we decided to create our own solution.

Our platform offers realistic practice tests that simulate the actual TOLC exam experience, allowing students to familiarize themselves with the format, timing, and types of questions they'll encounter on test day.

Whether you're preparing for TOLC-I (Engineering, Science and Technology) or TOLC-E (Economics, Business and Statistics), our platform provides the resources you need to practice effectively and boost your confidence.

What started as a small project has grown into a comprehensive platform helping thousands of students achieve their academic goals and gain admission to their desired university programs across Italy.

Platform Overview

What we offer to help you succeed

Comprehensive TOLC Preparation

Our platform is designed to provide a complete preparation experience for both TOLC-I and TOLC-E exams. We've carefully analyzed the official exam structure and created a system that replicates the real testing environment.

  • Authentic Exam Simulation: Experience tests that mirror the real TOLC format, interface, and question types.
  • Comprehensive Question Bank: Practice with hundreds of questions across all TOLC sections.
  • Detailed Performance Analysis: Receive comprehensive feedback on your performance.
  • Downloadable Results: Save your test results as PDFs for future reference.
  • Educational Blog: Access articles and tips about TOLC preparation and university admissions.

How Our Platform Works

A step-by-step guide to using TOLC Mock Test

1

Choose Your Exam Type

Select between TOLC-I (Engineering, Science and Technology) or TOLC-E (Economics, Business and Statistics) based on your university program requirements.

2

Take Mock Tests

Experience realistic exam conditions with our timed mock tests in a distraction-free, full-screen environment that simulates the actual TOLC testing interface.

3

Review Your Results

After completing a test, receive detailed feedback on your performance, including scores by section, correct and incorrect answers, and explanations for each question.

4

Analyze Your Performance

Identify your strengths and weaknesses in different sections and topics to focus your study efforts more effectively and efficiently.

5

Download PDF Results

Save and review your test results anytime by downloading them in PDF format. Use these reports to track your progress over time.
